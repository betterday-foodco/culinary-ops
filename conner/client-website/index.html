From the founders · Calgary, AB

We knew how we
wanted to eat.
Life had other plans.

Between two full-time jobs in teaching and social work, a youngster and another on the way, cooking the way our bodies actually craved felt impossible. Takeout was unsustainable. Shopping and cooking pushed us over the top.

So we built the thing we wished existed — meals that give you the time and fuel you actually need. Food worth feeding your kids.

If you've ever felt pulled in every direction but the kitchen — BetterDay was built for you.
